Content writing

I have several hobbies, some of which align with my professional field, while others serve as a source of positive energy and a way to break free from the work and computer environment. These activities are crucial to maintaining a healthy balance, preventing physical deterioration, and ensuring I remain strong and active over time.

I have developed a passion for creating content related to Arabic voiceovers and storytelling. You can find these works on my second channel @JISAN, which boasts an audience of sixty thousand followers. This work demands a strong understanding of narrative style, adjusting layers of sound according to each literary situation, and proficiency in spelling, comprehension , and narration. It involves precisely tuning the audio layers to the events of the story , immersing the viewer's emotions deeply into the fabric of each scene. Engaging in this work brings me comfort, as whenever I find myself with free time, I dedicate it to editing a piece, showcasing my talent in editing and voice narration. I plan to leverage this skill in e-commerce in the near future.

Soccer

Swimming is not just a physical activity for me; it's a source of joy and tranquility. The sensation of gliding through the water, the rhythmic strokes, and the feeling of weightlessness create a unique experience that I find both invigorating and calming. Whether I'm swimming laps for exercise or simply floating to unwind, the water has a therapeutic effect on both my body and mind. It's a form of self-expression, a way to stay fit, and, most importantly, a passion that brings immense pleasure to my life.

Football is my passion, a love affair that began in my childhood and has become an integral part of my life. I was a skilled player with strong abilities in using my left foot. I had a unique talent for ball control and scoring stunning goals. However, an injury to my knee and back came as a powerful shock to me and my family. This injury forced me to stop playing and leave the field I loved. Despite this setback, my love for the game and my interest in football remained alive. The beautiful memories of playing days continue to be an unforgettable part of my journey.
